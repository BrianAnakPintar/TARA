What is TARA?

This is a simple web app, meant to be used to help new students gain more information about the teachers inside of our school. We spent a lot of time working on this app so feel free to message any of us if you have any feedback on how to improve our app :)

Who are we?

We are a group of like-minded individuals who wants to better our school and it's future students by allowing them to have an idea of the state of the current teachers in our school.

Contact Us!

We are available on a variety of social media, if you have any questions or would like to report an issue feel free to message one of us
